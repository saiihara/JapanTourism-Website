Recommended routes and how to move across Japan

Why are routes recommended

Scenic Beauty: As an island nation with nearly three-quarters of its terrain covered in mountains, Japan is ideal for scenic road trips. The country offers excellent roads, incredible scenery, towering mountains, and vibrant cities.

Cultural immersion: These routes also offer opportunities to immerse oneself in local culture. For example, the route from Izu Peninsula to Hakone passes through historic port towns and offers views of the Pacific.

Diverse Experiences: The routes allow travelers to experience a variety of landscapes and activities. For example, The Shimanami Kaidō route provides scenic island-hopping via suspension bridges.

Contrasting Landscapes: Japan is a land of contrasts between its deep-rooted traditions and futuristic cities4. This contrast can be experienced firsthand when traveling through different routes in the country without a ruote or map

The Japan Rail Pass offers unlimited access to most of the Japan Rail Group’s facilities, including high-speed bullet trains, local trains, certain bus lines, and ferry services.

It allows for free reservations and seat selection, and offers discounts on car rentals and hotels. This can make your travel experience in Japan more convenient and potentially more cost-effective.

In addition, the Japan Rail Pass can be a great way to experience the efficiency and punctuality of Japan’s world-renowned railway system, making your journey through the country a smooth and enjoyable one
