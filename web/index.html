Ueno park

Ueno Park is a large public park located in the Ueno district of Taito, Tokyo, Japan. Renowned for its cherry blossoms in spring, the park is home to various museums, including the Tokyo National Museum and the Ueno Zoo. Boasting scenic ponds, temples, and historical monuments, Ueno Park is a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking cultural, recreational, and natural experiences in the heart of Tokyo.

Buy Tickets

Toji Temple

Toji Temple, located in Kyoto, Japan, is renowned for its towering five-story pagoda, the tallest wooden structure of its kind in the country. Founded in the 8th century and designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, the temple is a cultural and historical gem, attracting visitors with its intricate architecture and seasonal beauty, especially during cherry blossom season. The temple stands as a symbol of Kyoto's rich heritage and spiritual significance..

Buy Tickets

Nikko park

Nikko National Park in Tochigi Prefecture, Japan, is a scenic reserve celebrated for its tranquil landscapes, vibrant autumn hues, and the UNESCO-listed Toshogu Shrine dedicated to Tokugawa Ieyasu. The park is a harmonious blend of nature and history, offering visitors a serene escape and cultural exploration. Home to the ornate Toshogu Shrine, a UNESCO World Heritage Site dedicated to Tokugawa Ieyasu, the founder of the Tokugawa shogunate.

Buy Nikko Pass

Yoshino Park

Yoshino Park, situated in Nara Prefecture, Japan, is renowned for its breathtaking cherry blossoms, particularly in Mount Yoshino, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The park attracts visitors during spring for its stunning cherry blossom viewing, offering a picturesque landscape of blooming trees. With historical significance and natural beauty, Yoshino Park is a cherished destination for those seeking the quintessential Japanese cherry blossom experience.

Official Site

Oze Park

Oze National Park in Japan is a vast wilderness renowned for its unique wetlands, mountains, and hiking trails. Popular for its picturesque landscapes and diverse ecosystems, the park offers a tranquil retreat for nature enthusiasts, with highlights including Ozegahara Marshland and Mount Hiuchigatake. During different seasons, visitors can enjoy alpine flowers in spring and vibrant foliage in autumn.

Access Oze park

Itsukushima Jinja

Itsukushima Shrine, on Miyajima Island in Hiroshima Bay, Japan, is famed for its "floating" torii gate. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the shrine is set against Mount Misen and the surrounding water, creating a picturesque atmosphere. Visitors can explore the main hall and vermilion bridges in this serene and spiritually significant location
